Hi, I'm Pascal Hartig.

I'm a Software Engineer at weluse & Open Source Addict.

I'm Pascal and I love building stuff. My favorite platforms for doing that are the web and mobile. On mobile my prefered environment is Android, which I built several native apps for. On the web, I like to use whatever is hip and trendy at the moment, but I always get back to my favorite language Python for the work behind the scenes.

I spend a lot of my free time doing Open Source. Two of my favorite projects I am working on are TodoMVC, a learning resource helping you select the right MV* framework, and Yeoman, a modern workflow for modern webapps. I'm also taking care of some of my own projects and those we open-sourced at weluse.
